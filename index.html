Marvel Ores Mod

This mod adds several of the metals in the MCU -
The 4 ores added are as follows
- Vibranium
- Symbiote
- Adamantium
- Carbonadium

Every ore has its own ingot/gem form along with armor/tool sets.
Adamantium for example can only be used for weapons (sword, axe).
Vibranium can be used for tools and armor and so on...
